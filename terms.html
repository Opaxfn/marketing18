1. Refund Policy and Contract Requirements

All refund requests are subject to the existence of a valid written contract between Marketing18 Inc. ("the Agency") and the client. Any potential refund must be explicitly agreed upon in writing within the contract terms that clearly specify the conditions that may trigger a refund or project cancellation.

Important: Without a signed contract that explicitly outlines refund conditions, no refunds will be processed under any circumstances.

2. Transaction Finality

All transactions with Marketing18 Inc. are considered final. If a client decides to discontinue working with the Agency, they may request that we:

  • Complete the remaining project deliverables
  • Implement all completed work
  • Provide assistance with exporting all assets and materials that were developed

3. Communication Requirements and Issue Resolution

In cases where there is lack of communication from the client's side, Marketing18 Inc. will make every reasonable effort to understand and fulfill the client's needs based on available information and project requirements.

If any problem arises that could potentially cause the client to request a refund or contract cancellation, the following process must be followed:

  • A formal meeting must be scheduled with the Agency
  • The Agency must be given reasonable time to address and resolve the issue
  • Documentation of the issue and resolution attempts must be maintained

Partial Refund Considerations: In exceptional circumstances where there is a demonstrable lack of attention from the Agency to fix critical issues (such as a website being down for multiple weeks without support), a partial refund may be considered based on the work completed and the severity of the service disruption.

4. Project Transfer Policy

If a client wishes to continue their project with another agency, individual contractor, or bring the solution in-house, Marketing18 Inc. can provide assistance with the transfer process. This may include:

  • Providing project documentation and assets
  • Technical handover sessions
  • Export of all developed materials and code
  • Brief consultation on project continuation

Important Note: Transfer assistance does not constitute grounds for a refund. All payments made remain final regardless of project transfer requests.
